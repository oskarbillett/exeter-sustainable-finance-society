Introduction to Rougemont Capital

Rougemont Capital is Exeter's student-run sustainable investment fund. We leverage the technical depth of the University of Exeter Business School to generate novel insights, combined with our commitment to sustainable finance principles to drive positive environmental and social impact through our investment decisions.

Led by sector heads, our analysts publish their top sustainable investment ideas in Rougemont Capital's research reports, culminating in our commitment to supporting companies that align with our ESG criteria and sustainable development goals.

Apply Here

Investment Philosophy

We aim to hold long-term positions in high-quality sustainable companies over a 1-3 year horizon, seeking to generate positive returns while driving meaningful environmental and social impact. Our approach focuses on identifying companies with strong ESG fundamentals, sustainable business models, and positive impact potential across geographies, sectors, and market caps within a diversified equity portfolio.

Governance Layers

1. Alumni Advisory Committee

Sustainable finance professionals provide portfolio oversight and feedback on investment decisions each term.

2. Investment Committee

Senior students with final accountability for sustainable investment decisions and ESG criteria compliance.

3. Sector Heads

Lead research process, mentor analysts, and drive coverage in sustainable sectors.

4. Analysts

Source ideas, build sustainable investment models, and deliver ESG-focused investment pitches.
